Buttermilk Biscuits

Golden brown, delicious, flaky, buttermilk biscuits baked to perfection

Ingredients

  • buttermilk
  • butter
  • salt
  • baking powder
  • baking soda
  • sugar
  • flour

Steps

  1. grease baking dish with butter or non stick spray
  2. add flour,salt, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda to mixing bowl and mix thoroughly
  3. add butter to the dry ingredients and break apart into pea sized pieces
  4. add butter milk and mix until a sticky dough forms with no pockets of dry ingreients
  5. tranfer dough to greased baking dish and pat down gently until entire bottom of dish is covered
  6. turn oven on at 450 degrees, once heated add the dish and cook for 30 mins rotating half way through
